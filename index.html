About

I am a Phillip Griffiths Assistant Research Professor at Duke University (jointly hosted by Di Fang and Yu Tong).

Before this, I was a Senior Quantum Research Associate at J.P. Morgan Chase. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Georgetown University in 2024, advised by Justin Thaler. Earlier, I completed an M.S. in Physics at Rutgers University and a B.S. in Physics and Mathematics at Arizona State University.
